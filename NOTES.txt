OpenGL 3.1 removed a lot of features, OpenGL 3.2 introduces two profiles:
'core' and 'compatibility' (core is only shader-based, etc)

iPhone and Android are OpenGL ES 2.0, which is a stripped down version
of Open GL that uses shaders
